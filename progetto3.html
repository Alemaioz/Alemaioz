Il Problema: Dare Voce alle Esigenze Umane

Many people want to live more sustainably but feel overwhelmed by where to start and lack community support. Existing environmental apps focus on individual tracking rather than building meaningful connections between like-minded individuals.

Obiettivi del Progetto

  • Create a supportive community platform for sustainable living enthusiasts
  • Simplify the process of finding and participating in local environmental initiatives
  • Provide actionable, personalized sustainability recommendations
  • Increase user engagement and long-term commitment to sustainable practices

Il Mio Approccio: Ascoltare, Comprendere, Agire

Ricerca Utente

Understanding the motivations, barriers, and daily experiences of environmentally conscious individuals was crucial to creating a solution that truly serves their needs.
